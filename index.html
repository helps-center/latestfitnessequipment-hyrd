**🏋️ FITNESS GEAR**

**1. Cardio Stuff**

These things get your heart pumping and help you last longer during workouts.

*   **Treadmill:** Walk, jog, or run. You can change the slope and speed.
*   **Elliptical:** Easy on your joints, like climbing stairs, walking, or running.
*   **Stationary Bike:**

    *   Upright: Feels like a regular bike.
    *   Recumbent: Sit back with support.
    *   Spin Bike: Super hard cycling, usually indoors.
*   **Rowing Machine:** Works your whole body by pulling.
*   **Stepper/Stair Climber:** Like climbing stairs, good for legs and butt.
*   **Air Bike:** The faster you pedal, the harder it gets.

**2. Strength Training Gear**

For getting stronger and building muscle.

*   **Free Weights:**

    *   Dumbbells: Work one side or both at the same time.
    *   Barbells: Use with plates, great for big lifts.
    *   Kettlebells: Good for fast movements and getting in shape.
    *   Weight Plates: Put on barbells or machines, come in different sizes.

*   **Benches:**

    *   Flat Bench: For presses, rows, and abs.
    *   Adjustable/Incline Bench: Change the angle.
    *   Power Rack/Squat Rack: Lift barbells safely for squats and bench presses.
    *   Smith Machine: The barbell moves on a track, good for safety.
*   **Cable Machine:** Uses pulleys for all kinds of exercises, change the weight easily.
*   **Resistance Bands:**

    *   Tube bands: With handles, work your upper and lower body.
    *   Loop bands: Great for your butt, thighs, and therapy.
*   **Medicine Balls:** Weighted, for fast movements and your core.
*   **Slam Balls:** Throw them hard without them bouncing back.
*   **Sandbags:** Weird weight that's good for real-life strength.

**3. Bodyweight Stuff**

Use your own weight to get stronger.

*   **Pull-up Bar:** Hang it up or get one that stands alone, for your upper body.
*   **Dip Station:** For dips, leg raises, and other bodyweight exercises.
*   **Push-up Bars:** Easier on your wrists and lets you go lower.
*   **Suspension Trainers:** Straps that use your weight, like TRX.

**4. Things for Moving Better**

Help your movement, coordination, and being quick.

*   **Battle Ropes:** Rope exercises for cardio and your upper body.
*   **Agility Ladder:** A ladder on the floor for practicing footwork.
*   **Plyo Box:** Jump on it for explosive leg workouts.
*   **Balance Boards &amp; Discs:** Get better at staying steady, good for your core.
*   **Parallettes:** Small bars for gymnastics moves.

**5. Recovery Stuff**

Helps with soreness, flexibility, and getting your muscles back in shape.

*   **Foam Roller:** Roll out your muscles to get rid of knots.
*   **Massage Balls:** Get deep into muscles.
*   **Massage Gun:** A tool that vibrates to massage you.
*   **Stretching Strap:** Helps you hold stretches, especially for your hamstrings and shoulders.

🧘 **YOGA STUFF**

Yoga gear helps you get into the right positions and makes it easier.

**1. Yoga Mat**

*   **What it's for:** A non-slip surface to stand on.
*   **What it's made of:** PVC, TPE, rubber, jute, cork.
*   **Types:**

    *   Travel Mat: Thin and light.
    *   Thick Mat: Extra padding for your knees.
    *   Eco Mat: Made from natural stuff.

**2. Yoga Block**

*   **What it's for:** Brings the floor closer to you so you can reach.
*   **What it's made of:** Foam (light), cork (hard), wood (really hard).
*   **Uses:**

    *   Help in standing poses.
    *   Lift your hips when sitting.
    *   Support your arms in backbends.

**3. Yoga Strap**

*   **What it's for:** Helps you reach further and get better alignment.
*   **How long:** Usually 6, 8, or 10 feet.
*   **With Buckle:** Makes loops, great for stretching your legs.

**4. Yoga Bolster**

*   **What it's for:** Soft support in relaxing yoga.
*   **Shapes:**

    *   Rectangular: More support.
    *   Cylindrical: Deeper backbends.

**5. Yoga Blanket**

*   **Uses:**

    *   Under your knees, hips, or neck for support.
    *   Rolled up for backbends.
    *   Good for relaxing at the end.

**6. Yoga Wheel**

*   **What it's for:** Helps with backbends, flexibility, and balance.
*   **Made of:** Hard plastic with padding on the outside.

**7. Yoga Chair**

*   **What it's for:** Support in poses.
*   **Used in:** Iyengar Yoga and resting poses.

**8. Eye Pillow**

*   **Use:** A weighted pillow to relax your eyes at the end of yoga. Usually filled with flaxseed or lavender.

✅ **KEEPING THINGS SAFE AND CLEAN**

**Fitness Gear:**

*   Wipe off sweat after you use it.
*   Check for loose screws.
*   Oil the moving parts.
*   Keep it in a dry place so it doesn't rust.

**Yoga Stuff:**

*   Clean mats with soap.
*   Let them dry.
*   Don't leave rubber mats in the sun.
*   Replace old blocks and straps.

🧠 **BONUS: Setting Up Your Space**

**Storage Ideas:**

*   Hang bands on hooks.
*   Put weights on racks.
*   Use baskets for straps and small stuff.
*   Put yoga gear on a shelf.

**Flooring:** Use rubber mats.

**Lighting:** Natural light for yoga, adjustable lights for the gym.

**Mirrors:** Check your form.

**Air:** Make sure you have good air flow.
**🏋️‍♂️ More Fitness Gear**

**6. Strength Machines (Selectorized/Plate-Loaded)**

These machines are nice because they let you work specific muscles with weights in a controlled way.

*   **Leg Press:** Works your quads, hamstrings, and glutes.
*   **Lat Pulldown:** Good for your upper back and biceps.
*   **Chest Press:** Like a bench press but on a machine.
*   **Leg Curl/Extension:** Works the back and front of your thighs.
*   **Cable Crossover:** Works chest, arms, and core. You can adjust the cables.
*   **Pec Deck:** Works your chest.
*   **Hack Squat:** Squats but with back support.

**7. Core Stuff**

These help you get a stronger core (abs and lower back).

*   **Ab Wheel/Roller:** Seriously works your abs.
*   **Sit-Up Bench:** Makes sit-ups harder (in a good way).
*   **Roman Chair/Hyperextension Bench:** Good for lower back, glutes, and hamstrings.
*   **Core Sliders:** Little discs to slide your hands or feet on for ab work.

**8. Grip &amp; Forearm Tools**

Because a strong grip is important!

*   **Hand Grippers:** Squeeze these to build grip strength.
*   **Wrist Rollers:** Works your forearms.
*   **Finger Bands:** Strengthens your finger muscles.

**🧘‍♀️ More Yoga Stuff**

**9. Yoga Sandbags**

*   **What they're for:** To help you feel grounded and supported in relaxing poses.
*   **How heavy:** Usually 5–10 pounds.
*   **Where to put them:** On your thighs, stomach, chest, or back.

**10. Zafu and Zabuton (Meditation Cushions)**

*   **Zafu:** A round cushion to lift your hips when you sit.
*   **Zabuton:** A flat cushion to put under the Zafu to cushion your knees and ankles.

**11. Wall Ropes/Yoga Wall Systems**

*   **What they're for:** To help with inversions, deep stretches, and getting your alignment right.
*   **Used in:** Iyengar Yoga and for therapy.

**12. Headstand Bench**

*   **Why use it:** Lets you do headstands safely without hurting your neck.
*   **What it is:** A padded, U-shaped bench.

**🛠️ What Stuff is Made Of (Yoga &amp; Fitness)**

| Item          | Materials           | Good                               | Not so good                     |
|---------------|--------------------|------------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Yoga Mat      | TPE, PVC, Rubber, Cork | Eco-friendly, lasts, doesn't slip | Some break down in the sun     |
| Blocks        | Foam, Cork, Wood     | Foam = soft, Cork = stable         | Wood = heavy, Foam = wears out fast |
| Bands         | Latex, Fabric        | Stretchy/ Doesn't Roll             | Latex breaks down               |
| Foam Rollers  | EVA, EPP, PVC core   | EVA = soft, EPP = firm            | EPP = less cushion              |
| Dumbbells     | Iron, Rubber, Vinyl  | Rubber protects floor, Vinyl cheap  | Iron is noisy, Vinyl is big       |
| Benches       | Steel + PU padding   | Lasts, easy to clean              | PU can rip                      |

**🏡 Setting Up Your Home Gym**

**🧘 Yoga Space**

*   Soft light, calming colors.
*   Enough space around your mat.
*   Props in baskets.
*   Mirror to check your poses.
*   Diffuser, speaker, and plants!

**🏋️ Small Home Gym**

*   Rubber or foam flooring.
*   Foldable bench, dumbbell rack.
*   Hang resistance bands on hooks.
*   Use wall shelves.
*   A mirror to check yourself out.

**🏋️‍♂️ Garage Gym**

*   Power rack, barbell, weights.
*   Bike or treadmill.
*   Space for kettlebells and ropes.
*   Good air and lighting.
*   Storage!

**🧽 Cleaning Tips**

| Item             | How to Clean                     | Pro Tip                                 |
|------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
| Yoga Mat         | Wipe with spray or soap           | Dry flat, not in the sun                 |
| Blocks &amp; Straps  | Wash straps, wipe blocks         | Don't soak cork/wood                   |
| Foam Roller      | Soap and cloth                    | Keep away from heat                      |
| Weights           | Disinfectant wipe                | Don't drop them!                         |
| Bands            | Wash cold, air dry                | Keep out of the sun                      |
| Cardio Machines | Wipe screen/handles with wipes  | Lube parts (check the manual)           |
