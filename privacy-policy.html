Privacy Policy

​Alright, here's a more human take on your privacy policy:

1. Intro

Your privacy is important to us, and we're serious about protecting your info. This policy explains how we handle your data on our real estate/rental site.

2. What Info We Grab

From People Looking to Buy or Rent:

*   Name, email, phone number, login info.
*   What you're looking for, saved properties, and questions you ask.
*   Times you want to see a place.
*   What you've said to agents or property owners.

From People Listing Properties (Sellers, Landlords, or Agents):

*   Name, business name, email, and phone number.
*   Property details, photos, and prices.
*   Messages from potential buyers/renters.
*   Payment info for better listings.

From Everyone:

*   IP address, device, browser, and what you do on the site.
*   Cookies and data to make the site better.

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your data to:

*   Set up and run your account.
*   Show listings and match people with the right properties.
*   Help buyers/renters talk to property listers.
*   Set up visits.
*   Handle payments for featured listings.
*   Send updates and deals (if you want them).
*   Make the site work better.
*   Stop fraud and keep the site safe.

4. Who Else Gets Your Data

We might share data with:

*   Payment companies (like Stripe, PayPal).
*   Hosting companies to store data.
*   Analytics services to see how the site's doing.
*   Email services for messages.
*   Legal folks if the law says so.

Everyone we share with has to keep your data safe and secret.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

*   Keep you logged in.
*   Track what you search for and like.
*   Make things personal.
*   See how the site's doing.

You can control cookies in your browser settings.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data while your account's active or as needed by law. You can ask us to delete your account anytime.

7. Your Rights

You can:

*   See your data.
*   Change your account info.
*   Delete your account.
*   Choose what emails you get.
*   Control cookies.

8. Keeping Data Safe

We're doing our best to protect your data. No system is perfect, though, so keep your password safe.

9. Kids' Privacy

Our site is not for kids under 13. We don't collect their data on purpose.

10. Changes

We might update this policy. The newest version will be here with the date it was changed.
